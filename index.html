We need to save our          Earth!
About this Website:



























Each year on April 22, people worldwide celebrate World Earth Day. It’s the day dedicated to protecting mother earth. People organize events, rallies, marches, concerts, and different projects to show their support for environmental protection. These activities aim to make the world aware of depleting conditions of our natural resources – land, air, water bodies, and forests. So in honour of earth day I have created this website.
